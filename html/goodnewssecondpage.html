June 2020 - Translator

My project was a English to Spanish/Spanish to English translator. I worked on this project with Leila last year. We used dictionary APIs from Merriam Webster to create a translator that will take a word in one language and translate it to the other language. As an expanding point from that, we would have started to try and make our translator translate whole sentences or phrases.
