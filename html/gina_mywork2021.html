Journal 2: Project beginnings
October 21th, 2021

Last year the buying and selling reccommendations were based on past changes with low and high candlesticks, but I think it would be more interesting to work on a method of predicting the future stock changes by finding patterns from the past. And then those future predictions will deterimen when the bot automatically buys and sells. It would also be helpful to have it calculate some form of accuracy, but I am not sure how that would be determined.

The first forumla I found is P/E x EPS = Future Price
P/E = Price/Earnings = Price to earning ratio
EPS = (Net Income - Dividend Payments)/ Weighted Average Outstanding Shares = Earnings per share

One thing I am strugging with is the dropdown content on my navagation bar. I can't get it in the right location. It's in the center but I want it to be right of the Home button. I tried using different floats, but it doesn't change anything except the dropdown content's position. The differences between and functions of float, display, and position are a bit confusing to me.
